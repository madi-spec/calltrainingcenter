## 2. Your First Training Session

### Step 1: Choose a Scenario

1. Click **Scenarios** in the sidebar (or use the quick action on your dashboard)
2. Browse available training scenarios
3. Each scenario card shows:
   - **Difficulty level** (Easy, Medium, Hard)
   - **Category** (Sales, Service, Retention, etc.)
   - **Estimated duration**
   - **Brief objective**

**Tip for beginners**: Start with "New Customer Inquiry" (Easy) to get comfortable with the system.

### Step 2: Pre-Call Briefing

After selecting a scenario, you'll see the **Pre-Call Briefing** page:

1. **Customer Profile**: Learn about who you'll be speaking with
   - Name, age, background
   - Personality traits
   - Current emotional state
   - Their situation and concerns

2. **Your Objective**: What you need to accomplish on this call

3. **Key Information**: Relevant company info, pricing, policies

4. **Tips**: Scenario-specific guidance

**Read this carefully!** Understanding the customer helps you succeed.

### Step 3: Start the Call

1. Click **Start Training Call**
2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
3. Wait for the call to connect (you'll see "Connecting...")
4. When connected, the AI customer will begin speaking

### Step 4: During the Call

- **Speak naturally** - The AI understands conversational speech
- **Watch the transcript** - See what's being said in real-time
- **Use the mute button** if you need to cough or pause
- **Watch the timer** - Aim for efficient but thorough conversations
- The AI customer will respond based on what YOU say:
  - Handle them well, and they'll calm down
  - Handle them poorly, and they may escalate

### Step 5: End the Call

1. When the conversation reaches a natural conclusion, click **End Call**
2. Wait for the analysis to complete (this takes a few seconds)
3. You'll be redirected to your **Results** page

## 3. Understanding Your Results

After each training call, you'll receive detailed coaching feedback.

### Overall Score

A score from 0-100 indicating your overall performance. Score ranges:
- **90-100**: Excellent - Expert level performance
- **80-89**: Good - Strong performance with minor improvements possible
- **70-79**: Satisfactory - Solid foundation, room for growth
- **60-69**: Needs Improvement - Focus on specific areas
- **Below 60**: Practice Required - Consider easier scenarios first

### Category Scores

Your performance is broken down into 5 categories:

| Category | What It Measures |
|----------|------------------|
| **Empathy & Rapport** | Connection with the customer, active listening, understanding their feelings |
| **Problem Resolution** | Effectively addressing concerns, providing solutions, taking ownership |
| **Product Knowledge** | Accuracy about services, pricing, policies, and procedures |
| **Professionalism** | Tone, language, representing the company appropriately |
| **Scenario-Specific** | Skills specific to this scenario (e.g., retention for cancellations) |

### Strengths

Green boxes highlighting what you did well, with direct quotes from the call. These reinforce good habits.

### Areas for Improvement

Yellow boxes showing where you can improve, including:
- What happened
- Why it matters
- Alternative approaches you could try

### Key Moment

The pivotal point in the call that most impacted the outcome. Understanding this helps you recognize similar moments in real calls.

### Next Steps

Actionable recommendations for your continued development.

## 4. Courses & Learning Paths

### What Are Courses?

Courses are structured learning paths with multiple modules that build skills progressively.

### Navigating Courses

1. Click **Courses** in the sidebar
2. Browse available courses by category
3. Your progress is shown on each course card
4. Click a course to see its modules

### Completing Modules

Each module contains:
- **Learning objectives**
- **Practice scenarios** (1-5 per module)
- **Completion requirements**

Modules unlock sequentially - complete one to unlock the next.

### Course Categories

- **Sales**: Converting inquiries to customers
- **Service**: Handling service requests professionally
- **Retention**: Saving cancellations and resolving complaints
- **Objection Handling**: Overcoming common customer objections
- **De-escalation**: Calming upset customers
- **Upselling**: Expanding service relationships

## 5. Gamification & Achievements

### Points System

Earn points for every training session:

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Complete a session | 10 base points |
| Score 90-100 | +50 bonus |
| Score 80-89 | +30 bonus |
| Score 70-79 | +20 bonus |
| Score 60-69 | +10 bonus |
| Easy difficulty | 1x multiplier |
| Medium difficulty | 1.5x multiplier |
| Hard difficulty | 2x multiplier |

### Streaks

Practice consistently to build streaks:

| Streak | Bonus Points |
|--------|-------------|
| 3 days | +25 |
| 7 days | +75 |
| 14 days | +150 |
| 30 days | +500 |
| 90 days | +2,000 |

Your current streak is displayed on your dashboard with a flame icon.

### Badges

Unlock badges for achievements:
- **First Steps**: Complete your first training session
- **Week Warrior**: 7-day practice streak
- **Perfect Score**: Achieve 100% on any scenario
- **Category Master**: Score 95%+ in any single category
- **Hard Mode Hero**: Complete all hard difficulty scenarios
- **Consistency Champion**: 30-day practice streak

View your badges on the **Leaderboard** page or in your profile.

### Leaderboard

1. Click **Leaderboard** in the sidebar
2. See rankings for your organization
3. Filter by timeframe: Weekly, Monthly, All-Time
4. Your position is highlighted

## 6. Managing Assignments

### Viewing Your Assignments

1. Click **My Assignments** in the sidebar
2. See all training assigned to you
3. Each assignment shows:
   - Scenario or course name
   - Due date
   - Current status
   - Progress (if multi-scenario)

### Assignment Statuses

- **Pending**: Not yet started
- **In Progress**: Started but not completed
- **Completed**: Finished successfully
- **Overdue**: Past due date and not completed

### Completing Assignments

1. Click on an assignment to start
2. Complete the required scenario(s)
3. The assignment automatically updates when finished

### Daily Practice Requirements

Your organization may have daily practice requirements:
- Check your dashboard for daily goal status
- Complete the required number of calls per day
- Maintain your streak by meeting daily goals

## 10. Troubleshooting

### Microphone Issues

**Problem**: Call won't connect or AI can't hear me

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure microphone permissions are granted in your browser
2. Check that the correct microphone is selected in browser settings
3. Test your microphone in your system settings
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Use Chrome or Edge for best compatibility

### Call Quality Issues

**Problem**: Audio is choppy or delayed

**Solutions**:
1. Check your internet connection (minimum 1 Mbps recommended)
2. Close other bandwidth-heavy applications
3. Use a wired connection if possible
4. Restart your browser

### Transcript Not Appearing

**Problem**: I don't see the real-time transcript

**Solutions**:
1. Wait a moment - there may be a slight delay
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Clear your browser cache

### Results Not Loading

**Problem**: Analysis takes too long or fails

**Solutions**:
1. Wait up to 30 seconds for analysis to complete
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try refreshing - your session is saved
4. Contact your administrator if the issue persists

### Can't Access Certain Features

**Problem**: Some menu items are grayed out or missing

**Explanation**: Access is based on your role:
- **Trainees** have basic training access
- **Managers** can see team data and assign training
- **Admins** can configure organization settings
- **Owners** have full access

Contact your manager if you need additional permissions.

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in or keep getting logged out

**Solutions**:
1. Clear browser cookies and cache
2. Try an incognito/private window
3. Reset your password using "Forgot Password"
4. Contact your administrator
