Print Instructions:

  1. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac)
  2. Select your printer (or "Save as PDF")
  3. Set margins to "None" or "Minimum"
  4. Enable "Background graphics"
  5. Enable "Two-sided printing" (flip on short edge)
  6. Print on card stock for best results
Note: Page 1 = Fronts, Page 2 = Backs. They're mirrored so when you flip the paper on the short edge, fronts and backs align perfectly. Cut along the lines.
